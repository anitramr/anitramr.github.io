Little Suzy

Little Suzy; she’s a prickly pear. Don’t rub her the wrong way or
you might get pricked. She’s a little fiesty so come prepared for
some attitude. Little Suzy wants someone who will take her to light
off illegal fireworks in the gravel pit. She enjoys tangling the
roots of other plants as well as growing where she isn’t supposed to.
Think you can handle her? Give her a try.
