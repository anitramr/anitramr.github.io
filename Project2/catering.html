Catering

Thank you for visiting us at Broder Catering, where we specialize in Nordic and non-Nordic fare. We're available to create custom menus tailored perfectly to your event needs.

Since 2015, Broder Söder has been partnering with Nordic Northwest to offer catering for special events, from weddings to corporate retreats to exhibits, at Nordia House in SW Portland. Over the past three years, we've expanded our on-site catering program to all Broder locations, including Café, Nord and Øst. Broder only offers on-site catering at this time, for room capacities and options please see below.
